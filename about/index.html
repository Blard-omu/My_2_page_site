About page

Blard Omu was born on 23rd November 2022 into the family of Omu Elechi in the city of Lagos, Nigeria. He started his early education at child best international school, Lagos. He is a graduate of the prestigous university of Lagos, where he obtained his first and second degrees in chemistry. He is an avid reader and researcher with special intrest in "computational chemistry" and the teaching and learning of STEM subjects. He's a Front End web developer and expert in curriculum development, manpower training and consultant. He is also TEF mentor (2021 Cohort 7), where he volunteered to help in the selection process of the Tony Elumelu Enterprenuership program.He is happily married and blessed with children.
